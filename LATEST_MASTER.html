FROZEN SOLDAT: OPERATIVE'S MANUAL

Master the combat loop, tactical assets, and meta-progression to survive the perimeter.

1. SURVIVAL & ECONOMY

  • The Loop: Defend against waves of enemies. Between waves, use the Armory Shop to spend Cash on health, armor, ammo, and tactical gear.
  • Elites & Mutators: Elites spawn randomly or via Wave Mutators (Blackout, Juggernaut, Swarm, Adrenaline). Elites have 2.2x HP but drop cash, ammo, health, and Perk Cases.

2. TACTICAL ASSETS (DRONES & ROBOTS)

  • FPV Drone: Deploy from your Right HUD. Once flying, your character is locked. Upgrade to the FPV Drone Bomber in the shop to drop up to 3 lethal bombs from above before detonating the drone itself!
  • Hunter Swarm (Mini Drones): Buy Swarm Packs in the shop. They passively orbit you and automatically break off to hunt and dive-bomb nearby enemies.
  • Spot Robot (Dog): A ground companion that shoots at enemies. You can issue Waypoint commands (tap the screen or use the gamepad interact button) to send Spot to scout locations. Upgrade to MK.II for 1.5x speed and 3x DPS.

3. PERKS & OVERCLOCKS

  • Perk Cases: Elites have a 10% chance to drop a blue Perk Case. Pick it up to pause the game and select a game-changing run modifier (e.g., Smart Munitions, Vampiric Rounds, Explosive Rounds).
  • Overclocks: Found at the bottom of the inter-wave Shop. Spend cash to permanently upgrade your weapons for the current run (Hollow Point rounds, Extended Mags, Fire Rate boosts).

4. PERSISTENT UPGRADES (INTEL)

  • You earn Intel by completing waves and eliminating Elites.
  • On the Main Menu, tab over to ARMORY. Spend Intel here to permanently increase your base stats across all future runs (Kevlar Weave for Armor, Cardio for Stamina, Offshore Accounts to retain cash on death).

5. CLASSIFIED: DEVELOPER MENU

  • Activation: On the main menu, rapidly click the "FROZEN SOLDAT" title text 10 times. A notification will appear.
  • Usage: During gameplay, press the Tilde (~) or F1 key to pause the game and open the Developer Command Center.
  • Features: Toggle God Mode, spawn specific weapons/enemies/perk cases, inject infinite cash, or force wave Mutators.
